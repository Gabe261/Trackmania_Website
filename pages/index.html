Trackmania

Trackmania 2020 is an arcade racing game made by NADEO and published by Ubisoft. Trackmania is a part of the Trackmania series, which includes 15 games, all with small iterations and improvements.

Trackmania 2020, the most recent game, contains a variety of singleplayer races, multiplayer races, daily multiplayer events and an in-game race creator. Hundreds of thousands of players compete for top finishing times and world records across all game modes.

Trackmania 2020 marks a significant change in the Trackmania franchise. Previously Trackmania games contained a set amount of single-player races and were sold at a fixed price. Now with Trackmania 2020, a new set of 25 single-player tracks are released every three months, official multiplayer events are held daily, and players can make and play custom tracks. Trackmania 2020 is free for download with different monthly and annual subscriptions allowing for additional game mode access. This allows Trackmania 2020 to foster a larger, more united community with a consistent revenue stream to keep the game running.
