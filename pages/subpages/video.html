These are two videos from Youtube (The creators and upload date can be found below the videos) that demonstrate the different types of road textures in Trackmania.

Creator: Calamity || Published: Jun 13, 2021

Creator: The Awesome Dragon || Published: Jul. 1, 2021

Descriptions

The top video takes place shortly after the new plastic road textures update. It displays the differences in acceleration between the regular road texture and the added plastic textures.

The video on the bottom is an overview of all road textures in Trackmania. The video goes over the strengths, weaknesses, and the best way to drive on each type of road.
